About

I am a fourth-year Electrical Engineering and Management student at McMaster University, with strong interests in controls, embedded systems, and robotics. I enjoy taking ideas from simulation and technical analysis into hardware that works in the real world.

Alongside my coursework, I’ve built hands-on experience through SCADA and protection & controls work, plus robotics and vehicle integration projects. My goal is to keep growing the skills that connect theory to reliable system behavior.
